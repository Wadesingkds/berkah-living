# LocalHub Brand Guidelines

## 1. Brand Overview

**Brand Name:** LocalHub  
**Tagline:** "Belanja Lokal, Mudah Digital"  
**Mission:** Memberdayakan UMKM lokal dengan platform digital yang mudah digunakan  
**Vision:** Menjadi marketplace pilihan untuk belanja produk lokal berkualitas  

### Brand Positioning
LocalHub adalah marketplace yang menghubungkan pembeli dengan UMKM lokal di sekitar mereka. Kami percaya pada kekuatan ekonomi lokal dan kemudahan teknologi digital.

**Target Audience:**
- Primary: UMKM lokal (penjual)
- Secondary: Pembeli lokal usia 25-45 tahun
- Tertiary: Komunitas yang peduli ekonomi lokal

---

## 2. Logo & Visual Identity

### Logo Concept
- **Symbol:** Location pin + house icon
- **Meaning:** Local marketplace (pin = location, house = community/home)
- **Style:** Modern, clean, geometric
- **Scalability:** Works at any size (512px down to 16px favicon)

### Logo Variations
1. **Full Logo** (logo.png/svg) - dengan text "LocalHub"
   - Usage: Website header, marketing materials
   - Minimum size: 200px width

2. **Icon Only** (logo-icon.png/svg) - mark only
   - Usage: App icon, favicon, social media profile
   - Minimum size: 64px

3. **Horizontal Lockup** - logo + text side-by-side
   - Usage: Email signatures, documents
   - Minimum size: 300px width

4. **Vertical Lockup** - logo above text
   - Usage: Social media, mobile apps
   - Minimum size: 200px

### Logo Clear Space
- Minimum clear space: 20% of logo height on all sides
- Never place other elements closer than this distance

### Logo Don'ts
- ❌ Don't stretch or distort the logo
- ❌ Don't change colors (use brand colors only)
- ❌ Don't add effects (shadows, gradients, outlines)
- ❌ Don't rotate or flip the logo
- ❌ Don't place on busy backgrounds without contrast

## 3. Color Palette

### Primary Colors

**Primary: Terracotta/Rust**
- Hex: `#C0633E`
- RGB: `192, 99, 62`
- Usage: Main brand color, CTAs, headers
- Meaning: Warmth, trust, organic, heritage

**Secondary: Golden Amber**
- Hex: `#D4A041`
- RGB: `212, 160, 65`
- Usage: Accents, highlights, prosperity indicators
- Meaning: Prosperity, light, harvest, abundance

**Accent: Forest Green**
- Hex: `#5C7C58`
- RGB: `92, 124, 88`
- Usage: Success states, organic/nature elements
- Meaning: Organic, health, nature, growth

### Neutral Colors

**Dark Text**
- Hex: `#1F2937`
- RGB: `31, 41, 55`
- Usage: Body text, primary text

**Light Background**
- Hex: `#F9FAFB`
- RGB: `249, 250, 251`
- Usage: Page backgrounds, cards

**Border/Divider**
- Hex: `#E5E7EB`
- RGB: `229, 231, 235`
- Usage: Borders, dividers, subtle separations

### Color Usage Guidelines
- **Primary (#C0633E):** Main CTAs, brand headers, key elements
- **Secondary (#D4A041):** Hover states, badges, highlights
- **Accent (#5C7C58):** Success messages, organic/local indicators
- **Neutrals:** Text, backgrounds, structure

### Accessibility
- Maintain minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 for text
- Test color combinations with colorblind simulators
- Don't rely on color alone to convey information

## 4. Typography

### Font Recommendations

**Headings (H1-H3):** Inter Bold / Poppins Bold
- Weight: 700 (Bold)
- Usage: Page titles, section headers, CTAs
- Line height: 1.2

**Body Text:** Inter Regular / Poppins Regular
- Weight: 400 (Regular)
- Usage: Paragraphs, descriptions, body copy
- Line height: 1.6

**Small Text/Labels:** Inter Medium / Poppins Medium
- Weight: 500 (Medium)
- Usage: Labels, badges, small headings
- Line height: 1.4

### Font Sizes (Web)
- H1: 32px (mobile: 24px)
- H2: 24px (mobile: 20px)
- H3: 20px (mobile: 18px)
- Body: 16px (mobile: 14px)
- Small: 14px (mobile: 12px)
- Tiny: 12px (mobile: 11px)

### Typography Don'ts
- ❌ Don't use more than 2 font families
- ❌ Don't use all caps for body text
- ❌ Don't use serif fonts (except special cases)
- ❌ Don't mix weights excessively
- ❌ Don't use text smaller than 12px

## 5. Tone of Voice

### Brand Voice Characteristics
- **Friendly:** Approachable, warm, welcoming
- **Clear:** Direct, easy to understand, no jargon
- **Supportive:** Empowering UMKM, helping buyers
- **Local:** Celebrating Indonesian culture & community
- **Honest:** Transparent, trustworthy, authentic

### Tone Examples

**For UMKM (Sellers):**
- "Jual produk Anda dengan mudah ke komunitas lokal"
- "Kami bantu bisnis Anda berkembang digital"
- "Raih lebih banyak pembeli dari sekitar Anda"

**For Buyers:**
- "Belanja dari UMKM lokal favorit Anda"
- "Dukung ekonomi lokal, belanja lebih dekat"
- "Produk berkualitas dari tetangga Anda"

**For Errors/Issues:**
- "Oops! Terjadi kesalahan. Mari kita perbaiki bersama"
- "Koneksi terputus. Coba lagi dalam beberapa detik"

### Tone Don'ts
- ❌ Don't be too formal or corporate
- ❌ Don't use slang excessively
- ❌ Don't be condescending
- ❌ Don't use negative language
- ❌ Don't ignore local context

## 6. Visual Style

### Design Principles
1. **Simplicity:** Clean, minimal, focused
2. **Warmth:** Inviting, human-centered
3. **Clarity:** Easy to scan, understand, navigate
4. **Consistency:** Predictable patterns, familiar elements
5. **Accessibility:** Inclusive, readable, usable by all

### Visual Elements

**Icons:**
- Style: Outline or filled, geometric
- Size: 24px (standard), 16px (small), 32px (large)
- Stroke width: 2px (outline), solid (filled)
- Color: Use brand colors or neutrals

**Buttons:**
- Primary: Terracotta background, white text
- Secondary: Outline style, terracotta border
- Hover: Darker shade of primary color
- Disabled: Gray background, gray text

**Cards:**
- Background: White or light gray
- Border: Subtle gray border or shadow
- Padding: 16px (mobile), 24px (desktop)
- Border radius: 8px

**Spacing:**
- Base unit: 8px
- Common: 8px, 16px, 24px, 32px, 48px
- Maintain consistent spacing throughout

### Photography Style
- Authentic, real-life images
- Local products and people
- Warm, natural lighting
- Diverse representation
- Avoid stock photos when possible

### Imagery Don'ts
- ❌ Don't use overly staged/artificial photos
- ❌ Don't use low-quality images
- ❌ Don't ignore diversity
- ❌ Don't use images that don't reflect local context
- ❌ Don't use copyrighted images without permission
