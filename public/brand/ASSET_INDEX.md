## Next Steps

### For Developers
1. Import color variables into your CSS/Tailwind config
2. Add Plus Jakarta Sans to your font stack
3. Use logo files from `public/brand/assets/`

### For Designers
1. Review `BRAND_GUIDELINES.md` for complete guidelines
2. Use mockups as templates for new materials
3. Export SVGs to PNG/JPG as needed for specific platforms

### For Marketing
1. Use social media post template for consistent branding
2. Follow tone of voice guidelines in brand doc
3. Reference color palette for all materials

## Export Recommendations

### For Social Media
- Instagram: 1080x1080px PNG
- Facebook: 1200x630px PNG
- Twitter: 1200x675px PNG
- LinkedIn: 1200x627px PNG

### For Print
- Business Cards: 90x55mm, 300 DPI, CMYK
- Flyers: A4/A5, 300 DPI, CMYK
- Banners: Custom size, 150 DPI minimum

### For Web
- Favicon: 32x32px, 16x16px PNG
- App Icon: 1024x1024px PNG (iOS), 512x512px PNG (Android)
- OG Image: 1200x630px PNG
